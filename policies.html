Policies

Contracts

Each project or working agreement is governed by a contract between Web Advocate and its clients. Specific policies are spelled out within the contract, but in general, the policies on this page are applied.

Payments

Projects with fixed prices are usually billed in 3 installments. The first installment is 30% and is due before work begins. The second 30% is due upon "proof" on "concept" approval. The final 40% is due upon project completion.

Projects or work that is billed hourly will be invoiced weekly or monthly, depending on the project.

Refunds

The first payment is considered a deposit and is non-refundable. Additionally, refunds are not given for work that has been completed according to the contract. The rights and methods for disputing a charge are given in the contract governing the project.

Cancellation

Rules governing cancellation are given in the contract. In general, clients may cancel a contract or work agreement at any time. Clients are responsible for paying for all work completed (which may be computed on a "percentage or work complete" scale or an hourly scale, depending on project.) See the section on Refunds, above.
